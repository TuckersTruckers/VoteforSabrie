About Sabrie

I didn’t decide to run because I wanted a title — I decided to run because I’m a mom. Our kids and schools shape every part of our daily life. I’m running to give parents a voice, support teachers, and ensure students feel safe and challenged.

With 4 of 5 children in the district, I’m actively involved — attending events, volunteering, and engaging with teachers and families. I bring professional experience as a social worker and counselor, combined with real-life parenting skills: organization, accountability, and problem-solving.

My family life is busy and relatable — evenings full of homework, school events, and activities. Being part of the community is central to who I am.
